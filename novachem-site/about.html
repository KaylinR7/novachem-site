Our Story

From Durban Roots to National Impact

Founded in the industrial hub of Durban, NovaChem began with a singular vision: to bridge the gap between complex chemical science and practical industrial application. We recognized that procurement officers and safety managers needed more than just supplies — they needed a partner who understood the rigors of the factory floor.

Today, we stand as a beacon of reliability, blending traditional manufacturing values with modern R&D to deliver cleaners, degreasers, and hygiene products that exceed international safety standards.
